# 🏛️ Cornerstone AI

> **Bridge Your Academic Gaps** — A personalized roadmap to help high schoolers (grades 9-12) close knowledge gaps and master what's next.

---

## 🎯 What Is Cornerstone?

Cornerstone is an interactive diagnostic and recovery platform designed to help high school students identify where they're struggling and get a **personalized, structured plan** to rebuild their foundations and move forward with confidence.

### The Problem We Solve

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Student tries new lesson...                        │
│                                                     │
│  ❌ Missing prerequisite knowledge                  │
│  ❌ Confused about basics                           │
│  ❌ Can't follow along                              │
│  ❌ Falls further behind                            │
│  ❌ Loses confidence                                │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### The Cornerstone Solution

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  1️⃣ Take the Diagnostic                            │
│     Answer a few questions about your course & level │
│                                                      │
│  2️⃣ Get Gap Analysis                                │
│     We identify exactly what's holding you back      │
│                                                      │
│  3️⃣ Receive Roadmap                                 │
│     Structured weekly plan with targeted tips        │
│                                                      │
│  4️⃣ Start Recovery                                  │
│     Follow the checklist, track progress, succeed ✓  │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔍 Diagnostic Tool
Tell us your course and how you're feeling. Our tool identifies exactly where the gaps are in your knowledge.

### 📋 Structured Timeline
Get a prioritized lesson plan broken down by week that tells you exactly what to review and when.

### 💡 Targeted Tips
Receive specific, actionable study tips for every milestone to maximize your learning time.

### 📊 Visual Progress Tracking
Watch your recovery progress update in real-time as you complete milestones.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Open the landing page**: Navigate to `Cornerstone/index.html`
2. **Click "Try the Diagnostic"**: Head to the academic roadmap tool
3. **Complete the form**: 
   - Enter your name
   - Select your current grade (9-12)
   - Choose your target course
   - Tell us how you're feeling
4. **Get your roadmap**: See your personalized recovery plan with priority gaps and upcoming units
